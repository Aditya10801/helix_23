Helix is a community of curious students, teachers, and parents who come together to explore the wonders of science. From hands-on experiments to engaging workshops, there's something for everyone at Helix. Join us on this exciting journey today !
Join the Helix science club and explore the wonders of chemistry, physics, biology, and more. Our team of expert teachers will guide you through exciting experiments and activities that will inspire a lifelong love for science.
Unlock the Wonders of Science with Helix. Helix is where young minds get to explore and experiment with science in a fun and engaging way. Our club is dedicated to fostering curiosity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills among students. With hands-on activities, workshops and competitions, Helix provides an enriching experience for science enthusiasts at Delhi Public School Rohini.

I Know It Better:

Students form groups of 2 to debate the accuracy of theoretical physical laws. They argue for and against a chosen topic from a list, and the most convincing team wins.

The next Wright:

Students design and explain paper planes for a distance flying competition, judged on flight distance and explanation quality.
