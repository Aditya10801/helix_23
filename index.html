About Us.

“Science is a way of thinking, much more than it is a body of knowledge”.

HELIX is the student-run Science club of DPS Rohini founded by Aryan Bansal and Shaurya Mittal under the guidance of Mrs. Deepika Sachdev and Mrs. Shelly Wadhwa. Through this club, we aim to feed the sparkling curiosity in students about the application of science in the amazing world out there. We aspire to demonstrate the practicality of concepts through our enlivening events. Nevertheless, we also hope to upskill teamwork and synergy amongst these young learners, thus inculcating a sense of professionalism in them.

Events.

Failing Physics!

Play your fav Marvel or DC movie and explain how what's shown is impossible in real life & fails the laws of physics.

Physics   Event Heads : Anirudh Dabas, Arnav Bhargava

Toycathon

Craft exciting toys & fun games based over scientific principles and explain their functionality in science terminologies.

Physics   Event Heads : Garvit Mittal, Parin Arora

I Know It Better:

Students form groups of 2 to debate the accuracy of theoretical physical laws. They argue for and against a chosen topic from a list, and the most convincing team wins.

Physics   Event Heads : Aadi Chhabra, Shaurya Gupta

The next Wright:

Students design and explain paper planes for a distance flying competition, judged on flight distance and explanation quality.

Physics   Event Heads : Bhavya Arora, Krishiv

Chematize

Dramatize & present a theatrical display on the chemistry concept of your liking using dance, musical instruments and what not.

Chemistry   Event Heads : Aanya Kaushik, Avni Tiwari

Hydro-Model

Prepare and assemble real-life models of hydrocarbons while telling interesting facts about those species.

Chemistry   Event Heads : Nimisha, Utsav

Magical Mania

In Magical Mania, students use scientific concepts and materials to create an extraordinary magic show. The participant who successfully combines science and magic to deliver the best performance is declared the winner.

Chemistry   Event Heads : Aarav Dua, Tejas

Nature Hunt:

Students form teams to participate in a nature exploration challenge. They follow a series of clues leading to a final plant. The first team to reach the final plant emerges as the winner.

Biology   Event Heads : Ishaan Dudeja , Divyaan Gupta

Deadly Creations!

Create your own micro-orgnism or virus that may start another deadly pandemic or even act as a life-saving antitode the fate of the world now rests in your hands

Biology   Event Heads : Ishita, Riddhima

Infocon

Be innovative and engineer an attractive Infographic on the topic of your choice whose design is informative as well as interesting.

Biology   Event Heads : Vrinda Shandiliya, Setuja Gupta
